Details

An eighth beef share is half of a cow, split 4 ways. Approximately 40-55 lbs. depending on the size of the cow. This does not happen exactly evenly as some cuts are more plentiful than others. What we guarantee is to split half of beef equally 4 ways.

Cuts Included

Shipped to your door will be some tenderloin steaks, ny strip steaks, rib-eye steaks, sirloin steaks, sirloin tip steaks, short ribs, chuck roasts, arm roasts, round roasts and ground beef. In addition, to round out your order, flank steak, brisket, or rump roast.
